Paragraph of My Summary of books

Wonder is a great book for new readers. It is about 400 pages long, but with a simple story. A Wrinkle in Time is a classic sci-fi book, and is about science, theory, life, etc. The next book in the series is The Wind in the Door, and finally, A Swiftly Tilting Planet. The Ultimate Hitchhikers Guide is a compilation of the Hitchhikers Guide to the Galaxy, The Restaurant at the End of the Universe, Life, the Universe and Everything, So Long, and Thanks for All The Fish, and Mostly Harmless.
